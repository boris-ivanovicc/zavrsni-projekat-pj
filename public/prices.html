At The Flock of Quills, every poem is handcrafted with care—but different voices bring different value. The price depends on the writer you choose, reflecting their style, experience, and the time it takes. Our writers are grouped into three tiers: Fledgling, High Feather, and Sky Crown—each representing their unique level of skill and artistry. Each writer uses a pseudonymous bird-inspired name, adding a touch of mystery and creativity to their craft. No matter the theme, our poets adapt their styles to bring your vision to life.
